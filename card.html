Humpback Whale

Only male humpback whales "sing." The song is the most complex in the animal kingdom.

  • Scientific Name: Megaptera novaeangliae
  • Average Length: 48 to 62.5 ft and 40 tons
  • Average Lifespan: 45-50 years
  • Habitat: Polar seas and all oceans

Named after the distinctive hump in front of their small dorsal fin, humpback whales often feed in large groups and are famous for their singing ability. Humpback whales make extensive seasonal migrations between high latitude summer feeding grounds and low latitude wintering grounds.
